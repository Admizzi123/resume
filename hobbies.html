3. Lifting Weights

In the back of our garage there is a small gym where my dad used to train. For some reason I never trained there growing up. It was always at my disposal yet I never used it. In 2021 and 2022 I was a chubby kid. The body dysmorphia I had when I looked at the mirror made me say that I needed to change.
In October 2022 I started to train regularly. It was at first cardio but then I started to incorporate weights and everything changed. Six months of consistence training letter I got a physique I was proud of. I still remember the day I saw a six pack after my whole life being chubby was the most fulfilling moment of my life. Now I train 4 days a week with a push pull leg routine followed by cardio at the end. I highly advise going to the gym or training in general since it will sharpen your mind and help you build discipline
